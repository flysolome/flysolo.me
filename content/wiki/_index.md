# FlySolo Wiki

FlySolo is the AI operating system for freelancers.

The first market wedge is experienced independent tech professionals who need to turn leads into signed, paid projects faster.

## Core Promise

Turn a lead into a signed contract and paid project with minimal effort.

## Primary Personas

- Freelance DevOps Engineers
- Software Developers
- Cloud Architects
- Security Consultants

## Differentiators

- AI proposal generation
- AI pricing suggestions
- AI contract generation
- End-to-end workflow
- Localized proposal and contract language
- Invoice workflow integration
