# Go-To-Market

Early FlySolo acquisition should not lead with "AI operating system".

Lead with the concrete job:

Van lead naar getekende opdracht, in professioneel Nederlands.

## Initial ICP

Senior Dutch-speaking freelance tech professionals in NL/BE:

- DevOps Engineers
- Cloud Architects
- Security Consultants
- Senior Software Developers

## Acquisition Motions

- Founder-led proposal and rate reviews, including €49-€99 paid review sessions credited toward Pro
- Dutch tech freelancer rate checker
- Opdracht naar voorstel generator
- Proposal confidence scoring
- Contract and scope risk checklist
- Moneybird invoice readiness checker
- Community proposal teardown sessions
- Recruiter and accountant referral loops

## Activation

The first conversion event should be a real opportunity:

1. Upload or describe a lead, recruiter message, or client brief.
2. Get rate guidance for the senior Dutch tech freelance market.
3. Generate a Dutch proposal or client response.
4. Move into contract, e-signature, Moneybird invoice, and payment after acceptance.

## Packaging

Professional is the default plan at €39/month. It should include advanced proposal generation, pricing benchmark, contract generation, e-signature, Moneybird sync, payment reminders, custom branding, and win/loss tracking.

The trial should be 14 days, no credit card required, with data preserved after trial.

## What Not To Do

Do not market broadly as AI for freelancers, sell agencies or recruiters first, overclaim legal compliance, treat Moneybird as the whole value proposition, or optimize for raw signups.

## North Star

Accepted client proposals managed through FlySolo.
