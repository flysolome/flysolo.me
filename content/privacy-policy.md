# Privacy Policy

FlySolo uses privacy-conscious defaults and only enables analytics storage after consent.

## Analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 to understand aggregate website usage, such as page views, device type, and referral sources. Analytics is configured with Google Consent Mode through tarteaucitron, so analytics storage is denied by default until you accept analytics cookies.

## Cookies

We use tarteaucitron to manage cookie consent. You can accept, deny, or change preferences from the cookie settings icon on the website.

## Contact

If you email FlySolo, we use your message and contact details only to respond to your request.

Contact us at hello@flysolo.me.
