FlySolo is an AI deal workflow for independent professionals. Its AI agent is Otto.

Otto helps freelancers respond faster, defend rates, clarify scope, and move from lead to signed paid project.

The product focus is the complete client lifecycle:

Lead -> Proposal -> Rate check -> Scope -> Contract -> E-signature -> Moneybird invoice -> Payment

The first useful moment is simple: use a real opportunity and leave with a commercial artifact you could send or export.

Onboarding starts with a recent or anonymized request. Otto helps improve the proposal, rate, scope, terms, and next step, then turns that learning into commercial memory.
