FlySolo is a Dutch-first AI deal workflow for senior tech freelancers. Its AI agent is Otto.

Otto helps senior freelancers respond faster, defend rates, clarify scope, and move from lead to signed paid project.

The product focus is the complete client lifecycle:

Lead -> Dutch proposal -> Rate check -> Scope -> Contract -> E-signature -> Moneybird invoice -> Payment

Activation means the freelancer uses a real opportunity and leaves with a commercial artifact they could send or export.

The first onboarding motion is founder-led: review a recent or anonymized request, improve the proposal, rate, scope, terms, and next step, then turn that learning into commercial memory.

Core personas:

- [Freelance DevOps Engineers](/devops-engineers/)
- [Freelance Cloud Architects](/cloud-architects/)
- [Freelance Security Consultants](/security-consultants/)
